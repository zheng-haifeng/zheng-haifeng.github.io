<h2>Professional Profile</h2>

<p>
I obtained my doctoral degree in 2022 from the University of Copenhagen, Denmark.
My research focuses on soil microorganisms and biogeochemical cycles, with particular
emphasis on microbial responses to climate change and microbial regulation and
feedbacks of the carbon cycle under global change scenarios.
</p>

<p>
I have published multiple first-author papers in leading journals in soil and
environmental ecology, including <em>Soil Biology and Biochemistry</em>
(3 papers, including one ESI Highly Cited Paper), <em>Functional Ecology</em>
(ESI Highly Cited Paper), <em>Geoderma</em>, <em>Ecosystems</em>, and
<em>Plant Ecology</em>.
</p>
